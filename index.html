Observed Physics

  • 1. Gravitational Lensing Notice how the back of the disk bends UP and OVER the black hole. Light from behind is pulled over the top by extreme gravity.
  • 2. Doppler Beaming The side of the disk moving toward you (left) appears brighter and blue-shifted. The receding side (right) is dimmer and red-shifted.
  • 3. Photon Sphere The thin ring of light inside the dark circle. Here, light is trapped in a circular orbit forever.
